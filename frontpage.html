About Me

Hello there! I'm Mike Liddy, and I'm on a thrilling journey in the realms of technology and nature. By day, I'm a passionate Full Stack Developer, weaving lines of code into digital wonders. But beyond the screen, I'm an adventurer and explorer at heart.

When I'm not immersed in the world of coding, you'll likely find me on a hiking trail, chasing the beauty of nature and soaking in the serenity of the great outdoors. Hiking is not just a hobby for me; it's a way to rejuvenate and find inspiration for my next coding endeavors.

Coding is more than a profession; it's a craft I practice with dedication. I love the challenge of solving problems and turning ideas into functional, elegant solutions. Whether it's front-end design or back-end logic, I enjoy the intricate dance of technology and creativity.

In my spare time, I'm a curious mind exploring the vast landscape of YouTube. From tech tutorials to nature documentaries, I believe in the power of continuous learning. It's a space where I not only gather knowledge but also share my experiences and insights with the community.

One of my most exciting ventures has been delving into the world of Artificial Intelligence. I wrote a 300-page book exploring the capabilities of AI, pushing its boundaries to unlock new possibilities. It's a testament to my fascination with the ever-evolving landscape of technology.

Join me on this multifaceted journey where technology, nature, and creativity converge. Let's embrace the wonders of coding, the beauty of the outdoors, and the endless possibilities that AI brings to our fingertips.
